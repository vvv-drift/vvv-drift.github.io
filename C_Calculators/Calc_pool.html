Hello and welcome to my yarn colour pooling visualiser/calculator. Please keep in mind this may recieve updates over time, feedback and testing is welcome!

All measurements are in CM!

Stitch Type/Length

Single Crochet assumes 1cm of yarn length per stitch, granny stitch assumes 2cm. This does not account for hook size or yarn weight, for the most accurate calculation please measure the length of yarn used in one stitch of your project and enter that into the third option.
